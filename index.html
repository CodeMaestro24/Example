Amboli Chaukul Retreat offers a blend of nature and hospitality, inviting visitors to explore its lush landscapes by day and enjoy tranquil evenings at Dark Forest Retreat. This hill station boasts hidden waterfalls, yet to be discovered temples, and opportunities for cultural immersion. It's become a popular weekend getaway for people from Goa, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, attracting couples and nature enthusiasts alike. Dark House Retreat provides comfortable accommodation for those eager to explore the flora and fauna of Amboli Chaukul.
